Samp

Description

Samp is a traditional South African dish made from dried, crushed maize kernels. It is usually slow-cooked until soft and often enjoyed with beans, meat, or rich stews.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups samp
  • 6 cups water
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp butter or margarine (optional, for flavor)

Instructions

  1. Rinse samp thoroughly in cold water.
  2. Place in a pot, cover with water, and soak overnight (optional but helps reduce cooking time).
  3. Drain, add fresh water, and bring to a boil.
  4. Reduce heat, cover, and simmer for 2â€“3 hours, stirring occasionally and topping up with water if needed, until soft.
  5. Season with salt and stir in butter if desired.
  6. Serve as a side dish with stews, meat, or chakalaka.
